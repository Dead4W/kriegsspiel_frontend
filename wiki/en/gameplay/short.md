# Gameplay: quick flow

Approximate flow for starting and running a match.

1. The umpire sends each player a link to their team room.
2. The umpire gives both teams their mission and briefing (for example: red team defends City A, blue team must capture it).
3. The umpire assigns troops to players (it helps to write exactly how many units of each type each player has).
4. Each player must spawn their general.
5. Players place their units and write initial orders.
6. The umpire starts the war using the **Umpire** button (top-left).  
   This copies player boards to the umpire board.
7. The umpire checks whether setup is valid and applies initial orders.

After that, the main loop repeats:

- the umpire advances time;
- sends reports to players;
- reacts to new player orders;
- clarifies conflicting or impossible orders when needed.
