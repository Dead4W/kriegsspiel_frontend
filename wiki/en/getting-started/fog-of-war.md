# Fog of war

In KRIEGSSPIEL Online 1880, you do not control an all-seeing army. You command through orders and reports, in the spirit of *The American Kriegsspiel*.

## Principle

- A commander acts with incomplete information.
- Orders move through the chain of command and take time.
- Reports also arrive with delay, so map knowledge can be outdated.
- Communications and messengers are vulnerable: information may be delayed, disrupted, or intercepted.

## How to play it

Plan actions through orders, read the battle through reports, and always account for uncertainty: exact positions of friendly and enemy forces become clear only after confirmed information.
